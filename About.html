About Carvan

Redefining the car buying experience through innovation, transparency, and exceptional service

Our Journey

Carvan began as a small, passionate team of automotive enthusiasts in downtown Detroit, dedicated to revolutionizing how people find and purchase their dream cars. We started with a simple mission: to make car buying transparent, enjoyable, and free from the traditional pressures of dealerships.

It soon became clear that modern car buyers deserved more than just a transactional experience. They needed a partner who could guide them through every step—from discovering the perfect vehicle to financing and beyond. We evolved from a simple marketplace into a comprehensive automotive platform.

At Carvan, we don't just sell cars—we match personalities with performance, lifestyles with luxury, and dreams with drive. We won't settle for 'OK' when 'extraordinary' is possible.

Today, Carvan offers an end-to-end automotive solution: virtual test drives, AI-powered vehicle matching, transparent pricing, and personalized financing options. We're committed to helping drivers find their perfect vehicle as seamlessly as possible, backed by technology that puts the customer in control.
